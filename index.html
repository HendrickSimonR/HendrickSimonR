About Me

I'm Hendrick, and I'm an Engineer

A Full-Stack Software Engineer to be exact!

I am a Developer with a background in education, writing, dance instruction and live performance. I combine my collective experience with programming to create memorable and compelling projects using clean, readable code.

The synchronization of dance and music flows similarly to coding. As one creates movement, so does a coder write to develop a functional program. When things do not flow, it is the individual's duty to work out the kinks in order to get things moving. (Pun intended!)

Dance is a creative and collaborative artform, and coding is one in the same! As a well-versed individual, I am committed to lifelong learning and progress, and I strive to impact the lives of others positively and with empathy through my works.

I hope you enjoy what I have to offer!
